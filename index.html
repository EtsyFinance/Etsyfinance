How to get your CSV from Etsy

1

Go to your Payment Account

In Etsy: Finances → Payment Account. Scroll to the bottom to find monthly statements.

2

Select a month & click CSV

Choose the month you want to analyze and click the CSV button to download it to your computer.

3

Upload it above

Drop it in the box above — your P&L, fee breakdown, and 1099-K estimate appear instantly.
